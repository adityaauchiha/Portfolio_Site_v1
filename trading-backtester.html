Algorithmic Trading Backtester

A Python-powered web application that allows traders to test trading strategies on historical market data, evaluate performance metrics, and analyze risk before deploying strategies in real markets.

Problem

  • Retail traders often test strategies manually without validating them on historical market data.
  • Many strategies appear profitable but fail when applied in real market conditions.
  • Traders lack tools to properly measure risk, drawdowns, and long-term performance.
  • There is no structured way to track and analyze historical trade results.
  • Poor strategy validation often leads to emotional decisions and financial losses.

Solution

  • Built a modular algorithmic trading backtesting engine using Python.
  • Implements a trend-following strategy using EMA crossover with RSI confirmation.
  • Includes risk management features like Stop Loss and Take Profit.
  • Supports multiple timeframes including 5 minute, 15 minute, and daily data.
  • Calculates professional performance metrics such as Sharpe Ratio, Win Rate, and Max Drawdown.
  • Displays a complete trade log and equity curve for strategy evaluation.
  • Helps traders test strategies and understand risk before deploying real capital.

Tech Stack

  • Python
  • Pandas & NumPy (Data Analysis)
  • Technical Indicators (EMA, SMA, RSI)
  • Streamlit (Interactive UI)
  • Matplotlib (Equity Curve Visualization)
  • HTML/CSS
